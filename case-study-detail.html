Our Thesis

In 2014, Vestar identified Environmental, Social and Governance (“ES&G”) as an on trend area in business and financial services, with favorable industry tailwinds were driving growth in governance and responsible investing solutions. We decided to participate in the sale process to carve ISS out of its former corporate parent, MSCI Inc. in an effort to pursue our interest in the space As a first time management buyout opportunity, we saw an ability to invigorate new business sales and operational efficiency as a standalone entity. Having established a strong brand with over 50% global market share, we believed ISS would make an excellent platform for expansion. The company also attractive business model, wit h h igh recurring revenue, retention rates and free cash flow profile. Lastly, we recognized the exceptional talent of the CEO and management team.

Vestar’s Edge: Relationship Building

Early on in the process, Vestar formed a close relationship with CEO Gary Retelny , developing alignment on the core thesis of establishing ISS as an independent entity and using it as a platform to pursue attractive industry growth opportunities. Vestar emerged from a highly competitive pool of buyers as management’s preferred partner to ultimately prevail in the auction. We utilized our experience with complex corporate carve outs to distinguish ourselves in the transaction process and the leadership team viewed us as a credible buyer with deep historical experience partnering with management teams to navigate successful carve outs from corporate parents.

Creating Value. Together.

Vestar and management worked closely together to establish ISS as a standalone entity, filling out the senior management team and putting the systems and infrastructure in place to accelerate organic growth. Together, we also completed five strategic acquisitions, expanding ISS’ capabilities in ES&G data and analytics and scaling its offering to both institutional investors and corporate issuers.

Vestar Advantage:

Given the complexity of the carve out and the incipient nature of the ES&G market, we initially anticipated achievement of our v alue creation goals would take nearly 5 years; however, the combination of Vestar’s strategic guidance and management’s strong leadership allowed us to achieve our shared goals earlier than expected. Under Vestar’s ownership, ISS successfully executed on the initial investment thesis, developing into an attractive, high growth platform with a clear market leadership position in ES&G.
