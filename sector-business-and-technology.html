Vestar has a long, successful track record investing in the Business & Technology Services sector. We are focused on market leading businesses with meaningful competitive differentiation and scalable operating models. We target companies in acyclical industries with attractive growth tailwinds and high barriers to entry.

Typically, our investments will exhibit high visibility recurring or reoccurring revenue streams, strong customer retention, and with high switching costs, and attractive margins and free cash flow conversion.
