Healthcare

Vestar focuses its healthcare investments on innovative companies at the forefront of enabling patients, providers, and payers to make better informed decisions which have an impact upon the cost and quality of care. We identify opportunities by starting with the larger macro, demographic, and regulatory context to inform key trends.

Today, we believe that information & data services or software & technology solutions present the most attractive investment opportunities in deploying this thematic approach . Across [more than 10] healthcare investments, we have unique experience scaling growth companies into market leading platforms.
